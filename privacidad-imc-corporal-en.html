1. Security Practices

All data collected or processed by this application is encrypted using secure protocols (such as HTTPS) during transmission and use. We do not sell or share personal or health information with third parties for purposes other than the operation of integrated advertising.

2. Types of Data Collected by This Application

In compliance with Google Play policies, we declare the collection and use of the following data:

  • 2.1. Personal Information: We collect the user's name solely for UI personalization purposes. This data is not shared with third parties.
  • 2.2. Health and Fitness Information: Weight, height, and pregnancy week data are collected for the app's core functionality (BMI calculation). This information is processed locally and is not shared.
  • 2.3. Approximate Location: Used exclusively for advertising or marketing purposes through third-party providers like Google AdMob.
  • 2.4. Performance Information: We collect crash logs and diagnostics anonymously to generate statistics and improve app stability.
  • 2.5. Device or Other IDs: Technical identifiers are collected for advertising, marketing, and fraud prevention. These IDs are shared with the Google AdMob SDK.

3. Processing Methods

Technical and advertising metadata processing is conducted through third parties (Google SDKs). Health-related calculations are executed locally on the user's device hardware to ensure maximum privacy.

4. Data Retention

Local Data: Calculation history and user profiles are kept indefinitely in the device's local database as long as the application is installed.

Third-Party Data: Analytical and advertising data are retained according to the privacy policies of Google AdMob and Firebase.

5. Data Deletion and User Rights

Users can manage their information as follows:

  • Manual Deletion: Users can delete individual records or the entire history within the app's settings.
  • Full Deletion: By uninstalling the app or clearing storage in Android settings, all local data is permanently removed.
  • Inquiries: For any questions, contact simplified.maed@gmail.com.
